Essential Safety Tips for Women

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these tips to stay safe in various situations:

1. Stay Alert

Be aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts. Avoid distractions like looking at your phone while walking.

2. Share Your Location

Share your live location with trusted friends or family members, especially when traveling alone or at night.

3. Emergency Contacts

Save important emergency numbers (e.g., police, ambulance, women helpline) on speed dial for quick access.

4. Avoid Unsafe Areas

Stay in well-lit, crowded areas, especially at night. Avoid isolated locations whenever possible.

5. Self-Defense Skills

Learn basic self-defense techniques to protect yourself in dangerous situations. Carry tools like pepper spray if legal in your area.

6. Secure Online Presence

Avoid sharing personal information online. Keep your social media profiles private to protect your identity.

7. Trust Your Intuition

If something feels wrong, it probably is. Trust your gut feelings and take necessary precautions immediately.

8. Safety in Rides

Always verify the driver and vehicle details when using ride-hailing services. Share trip details with a friend or family member.

9. Know Emergency Exits

When visiting new places like malls or hotels, locate emergency exits to prepare for any unforeseen situations.

10. Trustworthy Companions

When going out, prefer being accompanied by trusted individuals. Inform someone you trust about your plans.
