Emergency Helpline Numbers

Save these important helpline numbers to ensure quick assistance in emergencies:

Women Helpline

1091

Available 24/7 for women's safety and crisis support.

Police Emergency

100

For immediate police assistance in emergencies.

Fire Department

101

Contact for fire-related emergencies and rescue operations.

Ambulance

102

For medical emergencies and ambulance services.

National Emergency Number

112

A universal emergency number for all types of crises.

Child Helpline

1098

Dedicated to protecting the rights and safety of children.

Domestic Violence Helpline

181

Support for survivors of domestic violence.

Cyber Crime Helpline

1930

For reporting online frauds and cybercrimes.

* All numbers are toll-free and available nationwide. Make sure to save these numbers in your contacts for easy access during emergencies.
